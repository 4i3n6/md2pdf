Privacy and Storage

100% Local. Your documents stay ONLY in your browser. Nothing is sent to servers.

Complete Privacy

  • 100% browser processing
  • Zero tracking or analytics
  • No accounts, no login
  • Works offline

Storage

  • Data in browser's localStorage
  • Limit: ~5MB per domain
  • MEM metric shows current usage

Backup

  • Use EXPORT MD to save files
  • Use COPY to copy content
  • Make regular backups!

Warning: Clearing browser data DELETES your documents.
