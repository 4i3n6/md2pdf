Getting Started with MD2PDF

Why does your AI write like that?

If you've used ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or other AIs, you've probably noticed that responses come with strange symbols like **, #, -, and `.

It's not a bug!

These symbols are Markdown formatting - a simple language that transforms plain text into formatted documents.

Markdown was created to be readable even without rendering. That's why AIs use this format: it's universal, simple, and works anywhere.

How to use MD2PDF

  1. Copy the AI text Select the entire response from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any other AI and copy it (Ctrl+C).
  2. Paste in INPUT_STREAM Open MD2PDF and paste the text in the left editor.
  3. See the real-time preview The right panel instantly shows how your document will look formatted.
  4. Export to PDF Click the [ EXP_PDF ] button or use Ctrl+Shift+E.
